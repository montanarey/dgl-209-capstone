What is Plant Parents?

Plant Parents is a web app aimed to support houseplant owners in their plant ownership journeys. It will allow users to create a digital collection representing their plant family, and use this to easily find care information on each plant. Users will also be able to set reminders for care tasks such as watering, fertilizing, and repotting.

Plant Parents is currently in development and does not have an expected release date at the moment.

    Plant Parents will offer the following:

  • Species-specific watering & fertilizing guidance
  • Propogation & repotting tips
  • Trustworthy lighting recommendations
